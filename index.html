About ID8 Fellowship

ID8 Fellowship

ID8 Fellowship, powered by ID8 Lab, is a 3 month program to train new imigrants and international students in Canadian universities to become an innovator and entrepreneur. The program has two components with 1 month of entrepreneurship training and 2 months of startup work placement.

ID8 fellowship program is open to new immigrants and international undergrad students in Canada.

ID8 Lab

ID8 Lab for Innovation and Entrepreneurship is a non-profit organization based in Canada to help immigrants starting innovative and scalable ventures. We offer best-in-class coaching, content, and community for our members to start, evaluate, and expand their ideas and businesses into North America.
